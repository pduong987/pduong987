How they started:

Queen are a British rock band formed in London in 1970. ... Before forming Queen, May and Taylor had played together in the band Smile. Mercury was a fan of Smile and encouraged them to experiment with more elaborate stage and recording techniques. He joined in 1970 and suggested the name "Queen".

Genre:

Rock, Classic Rock,Synth-pop

Controversy:

Queen may not have been the most squeaky-clean band ever (partially thanks to Freddie Mercury's antics) but some fans might not know they made a music video so controversial it was banned from MTV. Surprisingly, a Queen music video which seemed innocuous in Britain was considered obscene in North America.
